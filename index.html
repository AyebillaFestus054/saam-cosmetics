Product Description

SAAM Young Serum is a premium skincare product formulated with nourishing ingredients designed to help promote healthy, hydrated, and radiant skin. It helps improve skin texture and supports a youthful appearance.

Key Ingredients

  • Argireline – Helps smooth the appearance of fine lines.
  • Hyaluronic Acid – Deeply hydrates the skin.
  • Alpha Arbutin – Helps brighten the skin tone.
  • Vitamin B3 & B5 – Nourish and strengthen skin.
  • Aloe Vera & Allantoin – Hydrate and soothe the skin.

How To Use

  1. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser.
  2. Dry the face with a clean towel.
  3. Apply a small amount of serum to face and neck.
  4. Gently pat until fully absorbed.
  5. Use twice daily (morning and evening).
